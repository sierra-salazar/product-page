Sumatra Coffee

Originating on an Indonesian island, this coffee is full of flavor and spice. The Sumatran coffee has been a favorite since its introduction in 1971.

It's a treasured dark roast with a muted acidity. This flavor is distinctive and bold with notes of rich herbs and rustic spices.

The Sumatra Coffee comes in three different formats: whole bean, ground, and K-Cup® Pods.
